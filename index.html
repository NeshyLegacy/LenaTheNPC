Who is LenaTheNPC?

LenaTheNPC is an extraordinary Twitch Bot that has become an integral part of the NeshyLegacy channel, thanks to the brilliant work of Kruiz Control.
The programming language is mostly Psuedo Code, but draws familiarities to JavaScript. This bot is a treasure trove of diverse features, offering everything from thrilling coinflips to thought-provoking Question of the Day prompts. Additionally, LenaTheNPC keeps track of each viewer's "First" Redeem, adding a touch of friendly competition to the channel.

The name "Lena" holds a special significance, as it is derived from the creator's beloved real-life dog, a charming Pomeranian X Japanese Spitz, she is very much beloved and a integral part of the community!She is a total Goofball, just like me!
